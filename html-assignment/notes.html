HTML Tags I Learned

<h1> - <h6>
Used for headings, from the biggest (<h1>) to the smallest (<h6>).
<p>
Paragraph tag, used for writing text content.
<img>
Used to display images on the page.
<a>
Anchor tag, creates links to other pages or websites.
<ul> and <li>
Used to create unordered lists with bullet points.
